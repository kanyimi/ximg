Scan coordinates – BETA version

The function allows us to scan coordinates on your .PNG and .JPG images.
The coordinates will then be available under each photo.

This function allows users to copy coordinates to the clipboard and paste them into their maps, eliminating the need to rewrite it manually!

This feature is currently in beta.

Therefore, we ask that you follow these recommendations:

  1. Use white font on a black background to write coordinates on the photo.
  2. Try to place the coordinates at the top of the image.
  3. Use a comma to separate numbers. Example: 52.516721, 13.383312
